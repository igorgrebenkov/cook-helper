CookHelper Instructions

Viewing/Deleting Recipes

To view the list of recipes, tap the Recipe List button from the main screen. A list of recipes will be displayed. To open a recipe, simply tap on its name.

To delete a recipe from the list, click on the Edit button in the top right corner of the Recipe List page. A list of checkboxes will pop up next to each recipe's name. Select the recipe(s) you would like to delete, and press the Delete button to remove them from the list.

To exit the delete recipe mode, simply press the Cancel button.

Editing a Recipe

An open recipe may be edited by tapping on the Edit button. This will make all the recipe's fields editable. To save your changes, tap on the OK button. To discard all changes, tap on the Cancel button.

Adding a Recipe

Recipes are added by tapping the Add button on the Recipe List page. A new window will pop up containing a blank recipe. Edit the recipe as desired, and tap the OK button to save your changes.

Searching for Recipes

To search for recipes from the recipe list, tap the Search Recipes button on the main screen. Searches may be performed by Ingredient/Category/Country.

The following boolean operators may be used to combine search terms:

  • AND
  • OR
  • NOT

When more than one boolean operator is used, precedence of operations MUST be indicated with parentheses. For example, to search for the AND of three ingredients,

(Ingredient1 AND Ingredient2) AND Ingredient3

is the required input. Note that,

Ingredient1 AND Ingredient2 AND Ingredient3

is not correct.

Similarly, for the AND/OR of three ingredients,

((Ingredient1 AND Ingredient2) OR Ingredient3) AND Ingredient4

is the required input.

Search results are sorted by the number of matching search terms. Recipes with the most matching search terms will be at the top, and the rest follow in descending order.
